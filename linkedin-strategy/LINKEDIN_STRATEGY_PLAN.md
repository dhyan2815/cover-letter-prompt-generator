## Step-by-Step Pipeline for a Viral Post

### 1. The Foundation (`niche-research`)
Identify what is *actually* happening right now. Don't guess. 
*   **Command:** `activate_skill niche-research`
*   **Goal:** Find 3-5 themes with "Strong Attention Signals" (Reddit/X stats).

### 2. The Hook Engine (`hook-generator`)
The first two lines determine if people click "See more."
*   **Command:** `activate_skill hook-generator`
*   **Rule:** 40 chars max per line. No questions in Line 1. Always use a digit.

### 3. The Authority Build (`post-writer`)
Draft the content using your unique voice.
*   **Command:** `activate_skill post-writer`
*   **Rule:** Reference `voice.md` to ensure you don't sound like generic ChatGPT. Avoid "Absence Patterns" (banned words).

### 4. The Quality Gate (`post-scorer`)
Never post a "7/10." Only post "9/10s."
*   **Command:** `activate_skill post-scorer`
*   **Goal:** Fix the CTAs and value density based on real benchmarks.

### 5. The Visual Booster (`gemini-carousel`)
For your best posts, create a carousel to double your reach.
*   **Command:** `activate_skill gemini-carousel`

## Key KPIs to Track
- **Reach/Impressions**: Are the hooks working?
- **Comment Rate**: Is the content sparking debate?
- **Profile Visits**: Is the `profile-optimizer` converting readers into fans?
